From Idea to Innovation

Portglint was founded in 2023 by a group of tech enthusiasts who shared a common vision: to create software that makes a real difference in how businesses operate.

What started as a small project in a co-working space has grown into a dynamic company with a clear mission to revolutionize the SaaS landscape. Our journey has been defined by continuous learning, adaptation, and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

Today, we're proud to be working with businesses of all sizes, helping them leverage technology to achieve their goals more efficiently and effectively.
