‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with Portglint products?

Getting started is easy! Simply click the "Try Free" button on any product page to begin your 14-day free trial. No credit card required. Our onboarding process will guide you through setting up your account and exploring key features.

Can I switch between plans?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, the new features will be immediately available. If you downgrade, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

Is my data secure with Portglint?

Absolutely. Security is our top priority. We use industry-standard encryption, regular security audits, and strict access controls to protect your data. Our systems are compliant with major security standards including SOC 2 and GDPR.

Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or educational institutions?

Yes, we offer special pricing for nonprofits, educational institutions, and startups. Please contact our sales team for more information about our discount programs.

What kind of support do you offer?

We offer multiple support channels including email, live chat, and phone support depending on your plan. Our knowledge base and community forum are available to all users. Enterprise customers receive dedicated account management and 24/7 priority support.
